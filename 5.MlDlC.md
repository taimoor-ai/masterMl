
# **Machine Learning Development Lifecycle (MLDLC)**

The **Machine Learning Development Lifecycle (MLDLC)** consists of **nine key steps** that guide the process of building a machine learning-based software product—from an initial idea to a fully deployed solution.

---

## 1. **Framing the Problem**

* Define the **problem** to be solved and identify  **target users/customers** .
* Calculate **costs** and determine  **team requirements** .
* Decide on:
  * **Supervised vs. unsupervised learning**
  * Suitable **algorithm types**
  * Sources of **data**

---

## 2. **Data Gathering**

* Collect data from sources such as:
  * CSV files
  * APIs
  * Web scraping
  * Databases
* Large companies may use **data warehouses** and frameworks like  **Spark** .

---

## 3. **Data Preprocessing**

* Raw data is often "dirty" and needs preparation:
  * **Remove duplicates**
  * **Handle missing values**
  * **Scale features** for algorithm compatibility

---

## 4. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**

* Analyze the data to understand  **relationships between inputs and outputs** .
* Techniques include:
  * **Data visualization** (graphs, charts)
  * **Univariate and multivariate analysis**
  * Handling **imbalanced datasets**

---

## 5. **Feature Engineering and Selection**

* **Create new features** to improve model performance.
* **Select the most important features** to reduce training time and complexity.

---

## 6. **Model Training**

* Apply various ML algorithms to the data to identify the  **best-performing model** .
* Train models from different **algorithm families** (e.g., tree-based, linear) for comparison.

---

## 7. **Model Evaluation**

* Assess models using  **performance metrics** :
  * Classification: **Accuracy, F1-score**
  * Regression: **Mean Squared Error (MSE), R²**
* Perform  **hyperparameter tuning** .
* Optionally use **ensemble learning** to combine multiple models for better performance.

---

## 8. **Deployment**

* Convert the finalized model into a  **binary file** .
* Integrate it into an **API** for usage in websites, mobile apps, or desktop software.
* Common hosting platforms:  **AWS, Google Cloud Platform (GCP), Heroku** .

---

## 9. **Testing and Optimization**

* Conduct **beta testing / A/B testing** with a small group of users.
* Perform operational tasks:
  * **Backups**
  * **Load balancing**
  * Schedule **retraining** to maintain model performance over time.
